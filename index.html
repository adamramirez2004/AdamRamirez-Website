I come from an analytical background. During my time in the Honors program at the University of California, Riverside I have taken honors courses to sharpen my writing and research skills. My analytical background also includes my time working at the Riverside County Office of Education conducting research and learning data analysis and visualization skills. Researching the economics of education is a personal passion of mine.

During my time in the Riverside County Office of Education in the Administration and Business Services Department I learned how the California education system is funded and managed. I also analyzed datasheets from the California Department of Education to research the correlation between student success rates and other variables such as poverty, funding per pupil, and unduplicated pupil percentages. This involved coordinating with LEAs, multiple RCOE Executive Directors, and the Deputy Superintendent of Schools.
